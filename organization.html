MaCCI IO Days

The MaCCI IO Day is an annual workshop for members of the departments of law and economics at the University of Mannheim as well as ZEW Mannheim to present current theoretical and empirical research in the field of industrial organization, in its full variety of shades and colors. I have been coordinating the Day since 2019.

  • May 10, 2019
  • October 9, 2020
  • May 28, 2021
  • May 27, 2022

MaCCI Law & Economics Conference 2019

The MaCCI Law & Economics conference series provides a platform for exchange and discussion among academic researchers and practitioners in public authorities, legal counsel, and economic consultancies. The goal is to stimulate and disseminate research in the field of innovation policy, competition, market design, and regulation. I co-organized the 2019 conference (the topic was “Intellectual Property Proceedings”) jointly with Lea Tochtermann, Department of Law at the University of Mannheim. Find the program here.
